Science involves systematically studying the natural world through careful observation and experimentation. It spans several disciplines, including physics, chemistry, biology, earth science, and astronomy, each exploring unique elements of the universe. Utilizing the scientific method—comprising steps such as making observations, forming hypotheses, conducting experiments, and analyzing results—science fosters technological progress, enhances medical treatments, and deepens our environmental understanding. Emerging areas like quantum computing and artificial intelligence hold promise for groundbreaking discoveries and innovative solutions to pressing global issues.
